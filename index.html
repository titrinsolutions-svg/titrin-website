Services

Our Services

Agrology & Compliance

Practical, regulator-informed agrology support to keep projects feasible and compliant on agricultural land.

  • Farm Plan (BC Ministry & ALC-compliant)
  • Land Capability Assessment (LCA)
  • Soil assessment & sampling report
  • Agricultural impact assessment
  • Environmental Farm Plan support
  • Agrologist stamp & sign-off

Permitting & Regulatory

End-to-end preparation and coordination for ALC and municipal approvals — built to reduce delays and revisions.

  • ALC non-farm use application
  • ALC soil/fill application narrative
  • ALR exclusion & inclusion support
  • Municipal soil deposit/removal permits
  • ESA Development Permit support
  • Regulatory response letters & revisions

Construction & Fill Management

Field-level delivery and oversight for soil/fill and agricultural land improvements — from sourcing to close-out.

  • Fill quality assessment & soil sourcing
  • Field inspection & oversight reports
  • RMA monitoring report (Year 1, 2, 3)
  • Closure & compliance report
  • Bond release support package
  • Construction coordination & sign-off
