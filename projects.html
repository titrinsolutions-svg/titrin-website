Our Projects

A selection of recent work across municipal infrastructure, agricultural compliance, and land development in British Columbia.

Projects
Development

River Road, Richmond – Rezoning & Soil Deposit

A complex rezoning and soil deposit application on an environmentally sensitive site in Richmond requiring multi-stakeholder coordination.

Our Role

Led the rezoning and soil deposit application, coordinated geotechnical, drainage, and ecological reports, managed stakeholder meetings and ensured fill and grading plans respected riparian setbacks and future road allowances.

Deliverables
Rezoning + soil deposit application narrative and submission package
Constraints summary (riparian setbacks, access, future road allowance)
Fill/grading rationale + compliance-oriented soil placement approach
Coordination of supporting reports (geotech, drainage, ecological/QEP)
Stakeholder/reviewer response support (meeting notes, revisions, response matrix)
Agricultural

Dajue Nursery – Greenhouse Development

A nursery needed guidance on greenhouse construction and soil improvements on challenging low-pH peat soils, plus regulatory approvals.

Our Role

Advised on greenhouse design and soil improvements for low-pH peat-based soils, handled soil removal applications and compliance issues, coordinated with municipal and ALC contacts to streamline permitting.

Deliverables
Greenhouse development advisory memo (site constraints + implementation)
Soil improvement recommendations for peat/low-pH soils
Permitting/application support for greenhouse and soil-related approvals
Regulator-ready documentation package + correspondence support
Construction/implementation notes aligned to compliance requirements
Remediation

LingYen Mountain Temple – Agricultural Remediation

A temple property required soil remediation on its farmland to resolve compliance issues with the city.

Our Role

Oversaw soil remediation on the temple's farmland, conducted final site inspection and drafted a closure report, liaised with city officials to confirm compliance and assist with bond release.

Deliverables
Remediation scope confirmation + field oversight notes
Site inspection records + photo documentation (pre/post)
Deficiency/corrective action list (as needed)
Closure report summarizing works completed and compliance status
Bond release support package aligned to municipal requirements
Agricultural

Athiana Acres – Richmond Farm

A Richmond farm required boundary realignment within the Agricultural Land Reserve, involving ALC and municipal coordination.

Our Role

Prepared an agricultural rationale memo for parcel boundary realignment, represented the client with the ALC and city staff, coordinated permitting and compliance deadlines for soil placement and site modifications.

Deliverables
Agricultural rationale memo supporting boundary realignment
Application coordination package (requirements checklist + submission-ready content)
Regulator liaison support (clarifications, revisions, correspondence)
Permitting/compliance tracker (conditions, deadlines, next steps)
Soil placement/site modification documentation plan
Municipal

The City of Colwood needed professional review and advisory support for an Agricultural Land Reserve exclusion application.

Our Role

Reviewed the land capability assessment and summarized regulatory context and findings, provided briefing notes and recommendations for the ALR exclusion, assisted staff with council report preparation.

Deliverables
Technical summary memo (land capability findings + implications)
Regulatory/context briefing notes for staff/council decision-making
Recommendations + constraints/risks summary
Council report support (draft language, edits, technical review comments)
Q&A / talking points for stakeholder and reviewer questions
Agricultural

Riverview Baseball Park

The City of Coquitlam needed soil analysis and root-zone specifications for a new sports field build.

Our Role

Conducted soil sampling and root-zone analysis, recommended and vetted sports-field root-zone blends for durability and drainage, reviewed gradation tests and provided stamped specifications to guide construction.

Deliverables
Soil sampling plan + results summary (construction-relevant interpretation)
Root-zone blend recommendations (performance + drainage suitability)
Review of supplier gradation testing / submittals
Stamped root-zone specifications for construction use
Installation/QA guidance notes (acceptance checks, practical controls)
